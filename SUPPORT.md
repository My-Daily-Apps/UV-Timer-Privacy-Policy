  ## Frequently Asked Questions

  ### Why does the app ask for location permission?
  UV Timer uses your location to fetch real-time UV index and weather
  data for your exact spot. Location data is sent only to the weather
  service to retrieve UV data and is never tied to your identity.

  ### Notifications aren't appearing
  Make sure UV Timer has permission to send notifications:
  **Settings → Notifications → UV Timer → Allow Notifications**.

  ### How do I add more saved locations?
  You can purchase additional location slots from the locations screen
  in the app. Three packs are available: 1 location, 5 locations, and
  10 locations.
