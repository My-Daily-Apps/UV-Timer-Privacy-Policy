  ## Frequently Asked Questions

  ### Does UV Timer have an Apple Watch app?
  Yes, UV Timer includes a Watch app and complications. Install it via
  the iPhone Watch app. The Watch shows your current UV reading, lets
  you start/stop the reapply timer, and surfaces severe weather alerts
  with a tap-through to the full alert on your iPhone.

  ### Are there Home Screen or Lock Screen widgets?
  Certainly, UV Timer offers a small Home Screen widget showing your current
  UV index, plus Lock Screen widgets in circular, rectangular, and
  formats. To add one, long-press your Home Screen or Lock
  Screen, tap the + button, search for "UV Timer", and pick the size
  you want.

  ### Why does the app ask for location permission?
  UV Timer uses your location to fetch real-time UV index and weather
  data for your exact spot. Location data is sent only to the weather
  service to retrieve UV data and is never tied to your identity.

  ### Notifications aren't appearing
  Make sure UV Timer has permission to send notifications:
  **Settings → Notifications → UV Timer → Allow Notifications**.

  ### How do I add more saved locations?
  You can purchase additional location slots from the locations screen
  in the app. Three packs are available: 1 location, 5 locations, and
  10 locations.
